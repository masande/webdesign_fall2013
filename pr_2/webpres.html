MUSEUM OF DIGITAL ART (MoDA)
The Museum of Digital Art is a virtual museum with a completely digital collection, so visitors can access galleries and digital art pieces online. The collection would feature images, experimental/interactive art, net art, video, and sound. MoDA would provide
a platform for the many contemporary artists working with media and ideas that aren't meant to be experienced in a physical museum setting.
The + module teselates into pixelated patterns that evoke MoDA's digital content.
The module also forms a checkerboard that relates to Photoshop as the visual language of digital art.
